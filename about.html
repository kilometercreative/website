About Kilometer Creative.

In June, 2017, Matt Vredevoogd and Kaeden Wile founded Kilometer Creative LLC with the intention of turning their hobby into a business. After 4 years of collaborating on projects throughout high school, an LLC was required to bring legitimacy and legality. The Corkboard app--a yet un-launched social media service for schools--was ready for its beta at Raisbeck Aviation High School except for a few legal check-boxes including Privacy Policy and Terms of Service. The subsequent creation of Kilometer Creative LLC brought with it a commitment to future development projects.

Our Passion for Technology.

In every new project, we try and incorporate a new technology, language, framework, or tool. Although it can be uncomfortable at times, by making learning a key pillar of our projects we ultimately expand our horizons and become better developers and designers. And because we end up trying so many different styles of programming, we're able to pick the best cutting-edge tool for the job.
